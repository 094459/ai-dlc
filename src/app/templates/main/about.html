About Fact Checker

Empowering communities to fight misinformation through collaborative fact-checking

Our Mission

Fact Checker is dedicated to promoting truth and transparency in our digital age. We believe that accurate information is the foundation of informed decision-making and democratic society. Our platform empowers communities to collaboratively verify claims, share credible sources, and build a more trustworthy information ecosystem.

How It Works

Submit Facts

Share information with supporting evidence. Include credible sources, context, and relevant hashtags to make your submissions discoverable.

Verify & Vote

Review submitted facts and vote on their accuracy. Help the community identify reliable information through collective verification.

Discuss & Learn

Engage in meaningful discussions through comments. Share insights, ask questions, and learn from diverse perspectives.

Our Values

Accuracy First

We prioritize factual accuracy over speed, ensuring that truth prevails over sensationalism.

Community-Driven

Our platform is powered by the collective wisdom and diligence of our community members.

Transparency

We maintain open processes, clear methodologies, and transparent moderation practices.

Impartiality

We strive to remain neutral and focus on evidence-based fact-checking regardless of political or ideological perspectives.

Our Approach

Community-Powered Verification

Unlike traditional fact-checking organizations that rely on a small team of experts, Fact Checker harnesses the power of crowdsourced verification. Our community members bring diverse expertise, perspectives, and local knowledge to the fact-checking process.

Evidence-Based Methodology

Every fact submission must include credible sources and supporting evidence. Our voting system allows the community to evaluate claims based on the quality and reliability of the provided evidence, not personal opinions or beliefs.

Continuous Learning

We continuously improve our platform based on community feedback, emerging misinformation trends, and advances in fact-checking methodologies. Our goal is to stay ahead of evolving disinformation tactics.

Our Community

Fact Checker is built and maintained by a diverse community of contributors including:

  • Journalists & Media Professionals: Bringing expertise in source verification and investigative techniques
  • Researchers & Academics: Contributing scholarly rigor and domain-specific knowledge
  • Technology Experts: Ensuring platform security, performance, and user experience
  • Concerned Citizens: Providing local knowledge and diverse perspectives from around the world
  • Educators: Helping to promote media literacy and critical thinking skills
