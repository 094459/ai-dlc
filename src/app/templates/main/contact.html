Frequently Asked Questions

You can report content directly on the platform using the report button available on facts and comments. For serious violations or if you need immediate assistance, email us at report@factchecker.com.

Active community members who demonstrate good judgment and follow our guidelines may be invited to become moderators. Continue participating positively in the community, and reach out to us at support@factchecker.com to express your interest.

We support research and journalism that promotes truth and transparency. Please contact us at media@factchecker.com to discuss data access, attribution requirements, and collaboration opportunities.

Our community-driven approach relies on multiple verification layers: source requirements, community voting, moderation oversight, and continuous improvement based on feedback. Learn more about our methodology on our About page.

For technical issues, please email support@factchecker.com with details about the problem, including your browser type, device, and steps to reproduce the issue. We'll respond as quickly as possible.
