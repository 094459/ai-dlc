Community Guidelines

Building a trustworthy community through shared values and respectful interaction

Welcome to Our Community

Fact Checker is built on the principle that truth emerges through collaborative verification and respectful dialogue. These guidelines help ensure our community remains a safe, productive space for everyone to contribute to the fight against misinformation.

Core Principles

Truth & Accuracy

Always strive for factual accuracy and provide credible sources for your claims.

Respect & Civility

Treat all community members with respect, regardless of differing viewpoints.

Transparency

Be open about your sources, methods, and potential conflicts of interest.

Collaboration

Work together to build a more informed and truthful community.

Fact Submission Guidelines

What Makes a Good Fact Submission:
  • Verifiable Claims: Submit statements that can be fact-checked with reliable sources
  • Clear Sources: Always include credible, accessible sources for your facts
  • Proper Context: Provide sufficient background information for others to understand
  • Relevant Tags: Use appropriate hashtags to help others find and categorize your content

Voting Guidelines

How to Vote Responsibly:
Remember: Your votes help determine what information the community considers reliable.
  • Fact: Vote "Fact" when the claim is supported by credible evidence
  • Fake: Vote "Fake" when the claim is demonstrably false or misleading
  • Research First: Always review the provided sources before voting
  • Stay Objective: Base your vote on evidence, not personal beliefs or preferences

Comment Guidelines

Constructive Discussion:
Do:
  • Provide additional sources or evidence
  • Ask clarifying questions
  • Share relevant expertise respectfully
  • Acknowledge when you're wrong
  • Use constructive language
Don't:
  • Attack other users personally
  • Share unverified information
  • Use offensive or discriminatory language
  • Spam or post irrelevant content
  • Engage in harassment

Prohibited Content

The following types of content are not allowed on Fact Checker:

  • Hate Speech: Content that promotes hatred or discrimination
  • Personal Information: Sharing private information without consent
  • Spam: Repetitive, irrelevant, or promotional content
  • Illegal Content: Content that violates applicable laws
  • Deliberate Misinformation: Knowingly sharing false information

Enforcement & Consequences

Violations of these guidelines may result in:

Warning

First-time minor violations

Temporary Suspension

Repeated or serious violations

Permanent Ban

Severe or persistent violations

Reporting Violations

If you encounter content that violates these guidelines, please report it using the report button available on facts and comments. Our moderation team will review all reports promptly and take appropriate action.

Note: False reporting or abuse of the reporting system is also a violation of these guidelines.

Questions or Appeals

If you have questions about these guidelines or wish to appeal a moderation decision, please contact our support team through the contact page.

Remember: These guidelines are designed to foster a positive, truth-seeking community. By participating in Fact Checker, you agree to follow these guidelines and help create a better-informed world.
